Q1: What skin type is Lancôme Longwear Foundation suitable for, and what pain points does it solve?
A1: It is a favorite for combination oily skin, precisely addressing six common pain points of this skin type: dryness on the U-zone, oiliness on the T-zone, heavy and stuffy finish, foundation settling into fine lines, patchy and fading wear, and a mask-like, overly pale look. Its skin-mimicking texture ensures the foundation adheres seamlessly for a natural finish, restoring the look of real, healthy skin. The starting price is RMB 479.

Q2: What makes its long-wear technology special?
A2: It features exclusive Dynamic Longwear Technology, which blends better with skin oil, resists fading from sweat, and stays smudge-proof against friction. Tested to withstand 10,000 facial movements without settling into lines, it holds fast against oil, sweat, and friction, keeping the base flawless all day long and looking even better as the night goes on.

Q3: How to choose the right shade, and does it have sun protection?
A3: The shades are exquisitely formulated for Asian skin tones, including PO-01 (Fair with Pink Undertone), PO-03 (Natural with Pink Undertone), P-01 (Neutral Natural with Pink Undertone), B-01 (Neutral Natural), and BO-03 (Natural with Warm Undertone). Each shade brightens the complexion by a subtle half-tone for a natural, non-pale finish. It also offers high sun protection with SPF40 PA+++, sufficient for daily commute protection.
