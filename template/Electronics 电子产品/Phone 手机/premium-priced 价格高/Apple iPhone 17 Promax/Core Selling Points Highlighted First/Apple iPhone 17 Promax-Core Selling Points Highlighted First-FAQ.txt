Q1: What upgrades does the imaging system of the iPhone 17 Pro Max have?
A1: All three rear cameras are 48MP fused lenses (wide, ultra-wide, telephoto), supporting 0.5x to 8x optical zoom for a wider focal range. It adds macro photography and a new Portrait mode with focus and depth control, and supports Dolby Vision 4K 120fps video shooting. The front 18MP Center Stage camera features Subject Tracking, ultra-stable video anti-shake and Dual Capture, making selfies and video calls smarter. The starting price is RMB 9,999.

Q2: What are the highlights of the screen and design?
A2: It is equipped with a 6.9-inch Super Retina XDR display, supporting ProMotion 120Hz adaptive refresh rate and Always-On display, with more convenient Dynamic Island interaction. The front is made of the second-generation Ceramic Shield with 3x improved scratch resistance, and the back is also Ceramic Shield, paired with a seamless aluminum unibody, available in three colorways: Starry Orange, Deep Blue and Silver.

Q3: How is the performance and battery life?
A3: It is equipped with the A19 Pro hex-core chip, and paired with iOS, it offers up to 37 hours of video playback. The built-in 4823mAh battery supports 40W wired fast charging and wireless charging. The USB-C port supports USB 3 with a maximum 20x increase in transfer speed, and also integrates SOS Emergency and Crash Detection for all-round safety protection.
