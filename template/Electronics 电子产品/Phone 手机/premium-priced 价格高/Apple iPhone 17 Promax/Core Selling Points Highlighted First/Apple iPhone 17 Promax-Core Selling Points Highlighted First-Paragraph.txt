iPhone 17 Pro Max centers on its Pro-grade camera system, redefining mobile photography. The rear 48-megapixel triple-camera system (main, ultra-wide, and telephoto) covers 0.5x to 8x optical zoom, capturing rich detail whether you’re shooting macro close-ups or distant scenes. The next-generation Portrait mode features focus and depth control, while Dolby Vision 4K 120fps video recording elevates creative possibilities. The front 18-megapixel Center Stage camera supports subject framing, super‑stable video, and simultaneous dual capture, ensuring your selfies and video calls always look their best. The screen and design are equally impressive. The 6.9-inch Super Retina XDR display with ProMotion 120Hz adaptive refresh rate and Always‑On display, paired with the Dynamic Island, delivers a highly interactive experience. The front features the second‑generation Ceramic Shield with 3x improved scratch resistance, and the back is also made of Ceramic Shield, all housed in an aerospace‑grade aluminum unibody design. It comes in three stylish colors: star‑orange, deep blue, and silver. Performance is driven by the A19 Pro six‑core chip, optimized with iOS, providing up to 37 hours of video playback. The 4823mAh battery supports 40W wired and wireless fast charging, while the USB‑C port with USB 3 enables up to 20x faster data transfer. On the safety front, it integrates SOS emergency calling and crash detection for around‑the‑clock protection. Storage starts at 256GB, with up to 1TB available, and the starting price is 9999 yuan. The iPhone 17 Pro Max combines professional imaging, a top‑tier display, and powerful performance into one device, establishing itself as Apple’s next‑generation flagship benchmark.
