The iPhone 17 Pro Max has a starting price of RMB 9,999, with 256GB of storage (up to 1TB optional), and is available in Starry Orange, Deep Blue and Silver. The body adopts a seamless aluminum unibody design, the front is fitted with the second-generation Ceramic Shield (3x improved scratch resistance), and the back is also covered with Ceramic Shield, balancing aesthetic appeal and durability.
The 6.9-inch Super Retina XDR display supports ProMotion 120Hz adaptive refresh rate and Always-On display, and Dynamic Island delivers an efficient interaction experience. The core of performance is the A19 Pro hex-core chip, which is deeply optimized with iOS and offers up to 37 hours of video playback. The 4823mAh battery supports 40W wired fast charging and wireless charging, and the USB-C port (USB 3) has a 20x faster transfer speed. On the safety front, it integrates SOS Emergency and Crash Detection to protect users in an all-round way.
In terms of imaging, the iPhone 17 Pro Max showcases Pro-grade capabilities. The rear 48MP fused triple-camera system (wide, ultra-wide, telephoto) covers 0.5x to 8x optical zoom, supporting macro photography, a new Portrait mode (focus and depth control), Dolby Vision 4K 120fps video recording, and new Photographic Styles, meeting diverse creative needs from daily use to professional work. The front 18MP Center Stage camera makes selfies and video calls smarter and more vivid with Subject Tracking, ultra-stable video anti-shake and Dual Capture. With a well-balanced configuration, the iPhone 17 Pro Max is Apple's new flagship of choice.
