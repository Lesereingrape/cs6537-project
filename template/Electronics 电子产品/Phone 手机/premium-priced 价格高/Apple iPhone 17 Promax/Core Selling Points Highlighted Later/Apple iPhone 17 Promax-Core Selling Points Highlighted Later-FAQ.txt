Q1: What are the screen specifications of the iPhone 17 Pro Max?
A1: It is equipped with a 6.9-inch Super Retina XDR display, supporting ProMotion 120Hz adaptive refresh rate and Always-On display, with convenient Dynamic Island interaction. The front is made of the second-generation Ceramic Shield with 3x improved scratch resistance, and the back is also Ceramic Shield, paired with an aluminum unibody for sturdiness and durability. The starting price is RMB 9,999.

Q2: How is the performance and battery life?
A2: It is powered by the A19 Pro hex-core chip and paired with iOS, offering up to 37 hours of video playback. The built-in 4823mAh battery supports 40W wired fast charging and wireless charging. The USB-C port supports USB 3 with a 20x boost in transfer speed, and integrates SOS Emergency and Crash Detection functions.

Q3: What are the core functions of the imaging system?
A3: The rear 48MP fused triple camera (wide, ultra-wide, telephoto) covers 0.5x to 8x optical zoom, supporting macro photography, a new Portrait mode (focus and depth control), and Dolby Vision 4K 120fps video recording. The front 18MP Center Stage camera supports Subject Tracking, ultra-stable video anti-shake and Dual Capture, meeting the needs of professional creation and daily recording.
