Q1: What are the display and performance configurations of the OPPO Reno14?
A1: The Reno14 is equipped with a 6.59-inch 1.5K small straight screen, featuring a 120Hz refresh rate, 3840Hz high-frequency PWM dimming and a 93.4% screen-to-body ratio, delivering delicate and eye-protective display effects. In terms of performance, it is powered by the Dimensity 8350 flagship chip (4nm process, over 1.4 million AnTuTu score) paired with the Tidal Engine, with a maximum memory of 16GB+1TB. ColorOS 15’s smooth dual engines ensure lag-free performance even with long-term use. It supports 8 hours of stable high frame rates for MOBA mobile games, delivering an outstanding gaming experience. The starting price is RMB 2,039.

Q2: How about its battery life and charging speed?
A2: It has a built-in 6000mAh long-life large battery, setting a new record for battery capacity in the Reno series. Combined with 80W Super Flash Charge, it enables fast recharging. The body is as slim as 7.32mm and as light as 187g, achieving both portability and long battery life. The Ice Crystal Heat Dissipation System (3x graphite heat dissipation capacity) ensures stable operation under high load.

Q3: What are the features of the Reno14’s imaging system and live photo functions?
A3: Its rear triple camera setup includes a 50MP Sony main camera (OIS), a 50MP periscope telephoto lens (OIS, 3.5x optical zoom, 120x digital zoom) and a 116° ultra-wide-angle lens, plus a front 50MP AF lens, covering all focal ranges for diverse shooting needs. The live photo functions are its core highlights: it supports high-definition telephoto live photos, flash live photos, 4K video to live photos, slow-motion to live photos and live capture, making every moment full of vitality. In addition, it also boasts standout features such as IP69 top-tier water resistance, trendy Mermaid design and a metal middle frame.
