The OPPO Reno14 is available in four colorways: Mermaid, Sun & Moon, Midsummer Green and Reef Black, with a starting price of RMB 2,039. The Mermaid colorway features the Flow Light Phantom craft, boasting iridescent fish tail textures that present ten distinct hues on a single device. The BabySkin soft-touch glass complements the Light Constellation Camera Module crafted from one-piece cold-carved glass perfectly. All models come with a metal middle frame (3x tougher) combined with cold-carved glass, and the body is an ultra-slim 7.32mm and an ultralight 187g, striking a perfect balance between aesthetic design and sturdiness.
It sports a 6.59-inch 1.5K small straight screen with a resolution of 2760×1256, a 120Hz refresh rate and a 93.4% screen-to-body ratio. The 3840Hz high-frequency PWM dimming ensures eye protection, while 1.07 billion colors and ZREAL certification deliver an immersive visual experience. At the heart of its performance lies the Dimensity 8350 flagship chip (4nm process, over 1.4 million AnTuTu score) paired with the Tidal Engine, along with a maximum storage of 16GB+1TB. ColorOS 15’s smooth dual engines (Aurora + Tidal) guarantee seamless multi-tasking. For gaming, it supports 8 hours of stable high frame rates for MOBA mobile games, HDR game graphics, Game Live Recording (one-click replay of 30 seconds of highlights and save as live photos) and Free Floating Windows. Combined with stereo dual speakers and an X-axis linear motor, it creates a fully immersive gaming experience.
In terms of battery life, the 6000mAh long-life large battery works with 80W Super Flash Charge for fast recharging, and the Ice Crystal Heat Dissipation System (3x graphite heat dissipation capacity) ensures stable and cool operation even under heavy load. It is fully equipped with connectivity features: infrared remote control, NFC, offline chat and O+ Cross-Screen Connection for seamless interaction with computers. Smart features including Xiaobu Memory (collect information with a three-finger upward swipe and access DeepSeek for Q&A), AI Intent Search and one-click screen query with Xiaobu Assistant greatly boost user efficiency.
The device achieves top-tier protection with triple IP69, IP68 and IP66 certifications, making it resistant to hot water, submersion and water jets. Its imaging system features a rear triple camera setup: a 50MP Sony main camera (OIS), a 50MP periscope telephoto lens (OIS, 85mm golden focal length, 3.5x optical zoom, 120x digital zoom) and a 116° ultra-wide-angle lens, plus a front 50MP AF lens. The ultra-bright intelligent flash with an independent telephoto flash provides professional fill light for all shooting scenarios.
The soul of the Reno14 lies in its industry-leading live photo features: high-definition telephoto live photos bring distant scenes to life, flash live photos light up night shots, 4K video to live photos extracts the perfect three seconds from videos, slow-motion to live photos adds fun to interesting moments, and live capture freezes dynamic instants perfectly. From hardware to software, the OPPO Reno14 lives up to its mission of "Amplifying the vitality of the moment" and is undoubtedly a true Live Photo Powerhouse.
