The OPPO Reno14 redefines the live photo experience as a "Live Photo Powerhouse". Its rear 50MP periscope telephoto lens (85mm golden focal length, 3.5x optical zoom, 120x zoom) supports high-definition telephoto live shooting for the first time, blending people and background naturally. Flash live photos capture bright moments even at night, while the 4K video to live photo function extracts the perfect three seconds from native 4K HDR videos to generate ultra-clear live photos. Slow-motion videos can also be converted into vivid live photos, making sharing more fun. Live capture relies on flagship-level double exposure, rendering dynamic details with exquisite clarity and delivering stunning frames for every cover.
In design, the Reno14 continues the trend-setting aesthetic. The Mermaid colorway features a flow light phantom craft with iridescent fish tail textures that shift with light, presenting ten different hues in one device. Color options including Sun & Moon (Moon Silver/Sun Orange), Midsummer Green, and Reef Black cater to diverse aesthetic tastes. The BabySkin soft-touch glass upgrades the crystal diamond craft, offering a gentle, baby-skin-like texture. The Light Constellation Camera Module adopts one-piece cold-carved glass, as transparent as amber and as sparkling as starlight. Paired with a premium metal middle frame (3x tougher) across the entire lineup and cold-carved glass, the body is an ultra-slim 7.32mm and an ultralight 187g, striking a perfect balance between sturdiness and portability.
It achieves top-tier protection with triple IP69, IP68 and IP66 certifications, resistant to hot water, submersion and water jets, handling rainy seasons and sandy dust with ease. The flagship imaging system is all-round powerful: the rear triple camera setup includes a 50MP Sony main camera (OIS), a 50MP periscope telephoto lens (OIS) and a 116° ultra-wide-angle lens, paired with a front 50MP super-sensitive lens (AF autofocus). Combined with an ultra-bright intelligent flash with an independent telephoto flash, it captures clear shots in all scenarios, near or far, bright or dark.
At the core of its performance is the Dimensity 8350 flagship chip (4nm process, over 1.4 million AnTuTu score) paired with the Tidal Engine, delivering low game latency and weak network optimization, and supporting 8 hours of stable high frame rate for MOBA mobile games. The 6000mAh long-life large battery with 80W Super Flash Charge ensures long battery life despite the slim body. A maximum 16GB+1TB large memory and ColorOS 15's smooth dual engines (Aurora + Tidal) guarantee seamless multi-tasking and lag-free performance over time.
For the display, it features a 6.59-inch 1.5K small straight screen (2760×1256) with a 120Hz refresh rate, 3840Hz PWM dimming, a 93.4% screen-to-body ratio and ZREAL certification, offering eye protection and an immersive viewing experience. The gaming experience is elevated with support for HDR game graphics, game live recording (one-click replay of 30 seconds of highlights), and free floating windows. Combined with stereo dual speakers and an X-axis linear motor, it delivers an all-round immersive experience for sight, sound and touch.
It is packed with rich smart features: Xiaobu Memory lets you collect information with a three-finger upward swipe, enabling cross-platform one-stop storage and access to DeepSeek for Q&A. AI Intent Search, one-click screen query with Xiaobu Assistant, and O+ Cross-Screen Connection for computers boost efficiency significantly. Thoughtful configurations including infrared remote control, NFC, offline chat, and Ice Crystal Heat Dissipation (3x graphite heat dissipation capacity) make the Reno14 a well-rounded trend-setting flagship, with a starting price of RMB 2,039.
