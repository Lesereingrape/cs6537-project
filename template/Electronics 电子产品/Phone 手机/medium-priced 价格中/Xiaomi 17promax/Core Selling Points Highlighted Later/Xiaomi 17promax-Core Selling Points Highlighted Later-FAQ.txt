Q1: What are the features of the display and design of the Xiaomi 17 Pro Max?
A1: It features a 6.9-inch ultra-narrow bezel large straight display, with LIPO 3D packaging technology greatly narrowing the bezels for a strong immersive experience. The screen has a 2608×1200 super resolution (9.38 million sub-pixels, full RGB arrangement) and an M10 light-emitting system with a peak brightness of 3500nit. It supports a 1-120Hz adaptive refresh rate, full-brightness DC dimming, Dolby Vision, etc., and has passed multi-screen color calibration. The body is available in four colors: Forest Green, Misty Purple, White and Black, with a 6-series aerospace aluminum alloy frame matched with a glass fiber back cover, and covered with Xiaomi Dragon Tempered Glass 3.0, which is shatter-resistant, scratch-resistant and fingerprint-resistant. The starting price is RMB 5,499.

Q2: How is its performance and battery life?
A2: It is equipped with the 5th Generation Snapdragon 8 Elite (3nm all big core, CPU +20%, GPU +23%, AI +37%). Combined with LPDDR5X+UFS4.1 and Xiaomi HyperCore chip-level scheduling, it delivers powerful performance. It has a built-in 7500mAh Xiaomi Jinshajiang Battery (16% silicon content, ≥80% capacity after 2000 charge cycles), supporting 100W wired flash charge (compatible with 100W PPS), 50W wireless flash charge and 22.5W reverse charging. The Surge P3+G2 dual chips enable precise management, with a DOU battery life of 1.74 days.

Q3: What are the highlights of the Mi Share Rear Display and Leica imaging system?
A3: The rear display supports selfie preview, dynamic notifications, sticky notes, avatar stickers, and control cards (music/calls/car control). Super Xiaoai can move selected content to the rear display, and album photos can be pasted to the rear display with one click. It can also play mini-games with a matching protective case. In terms of imaging, it has a rear Leica professional triple camera: main camera (50MP, f/1.67, LOFIC high dynamic range, 16.5EV, OIS), telephoto lens (50MP, 5x optical zoom, 30cm macro, OIS), ultra-wide-angle lens (50MP, 102°, 5cm macro). The full-set optical coatings eliminate flare and ghosting, the new generation Master Portrait Algorithm ensures clear details in backlight, and it supports 0.7~120x zoom.
