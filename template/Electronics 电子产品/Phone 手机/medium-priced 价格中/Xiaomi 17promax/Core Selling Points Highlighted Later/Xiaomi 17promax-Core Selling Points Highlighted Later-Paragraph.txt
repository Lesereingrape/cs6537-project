The Xiaomi 17 Pro Max starts at RMB 5,499 and is available in four colors: Forest Green, Misty Purple, White and Black, making a stunning debut with a 6.9-inch ultra-narrow bezel large straight display. The screen adopts LIPO 3D packaging technology with extremely narrow bezels, bringing a strong immersive experience the moment it is lit up. The 2608×1200 super resolution (9.38 million sub-pixels, full RGB arrangement) presents sharp details, and the M10 light-emitting system has a peak brightness of 3500nit. The 1-120Hz adaptive refresh rate and full-brightness DC dimming balance smoothness and eye protection, while Dolby Vision, HDR Vivid and multi-screen color calibration deliver true and professional colors. The body is covered with Xiaomi Dragon Tempered Glass 3.0, which is shatter and scratch resistant. The 6-series aerospace aluminum alloy frame is matched with a glass fiber back cover with an anti-fingerprint coating, combining excellent hand feel and durability.
At the core of its performance is the 5th Generation Snapdragon 8 Elite, adopting the 3rd generation 3nm process and a 2+6 all big core design, with CPU, GPU and AI performance improved by 20%, 23% and 37% respectively. Combined with LPDDR5X and UFS4.1 high-speed storage, Xiaomi HyperCore chip-level scheduling ensures full-link power consumption management. The 7500mAh Xiaomi Jinshajiang Battery has a silicon content of up to 16%, leading in energy density, with a capacity of ≥80% after 2000 charge cycles. It supports 100W Surge Wired Flash Charge (compatible with 100W PPS), 50W Wireless Flash Charge and 22.5W reverse charging. The Surge P3+G2 dual chips achieve precise management, with a DOU battery life of 1.74 days and excellent low-temperature performance.
The Mi Share Rear Display breaks the tradition and endows the phone with more creative possibilities. It supports real-time selfie preview with the rear main camera, and the imaging quality is greatly improved with the beauty algorithm; dynamic notifications display ride-hailing, food delivery and flight information in real time; sticky notes and personalized wallpapers can be set at will, with a variety of templates available for the avatar sticker mode, and it supports one-click printing by connecting to a Mijia printer. Super Xiaoai can directly paste the content selected on the front display or album photos to the rear display, and the rear display control cards enable music switching, call answering and remote car control (vehicle connection support required). Matched with a retro handheld game console protective case, the rear display instantly turns into a casual game console, full of fun.
The Leica imaging system continues to deepen professional photography. The rear triple camera includes a 50MP Leica Ultra High Dynamic Range Main Camera (Light Hunter 950L, f/1.67 large aperture, LOFIC high dynamic range technology, 16.5EV dynamic range, OIS), a 50MP Leica Super Light-Gathering Periscope Telephoto (1/2-inch large sensor, f/2.6, 5x optical zoom, 30cm telephoto macro, OIS) and a 50MP Leica Ultra-Wide-Angle (102° field of view, 5cm super macro). Leica's full-set optical coatings (MgF2/LSC/ALD/PVD/SWC) significantly optimize curved flare, reduce picture haze, and ensure pure and transparent backlight shooting. Driven by ultra-high computing power, the new generation Master Portrait Algorithm delivers true skin tones and clear details in backlight. It supports 0.7~120x zoom for photography and 0.7~15x zoom for video, and the 50MP front camera also delivers excellent performance.
On the system level, Xiaomi HyperOS 3 brings a smooth and user-friendly full ecosystem experience for people, cars and homes, with upgraded interconnection with Apple devices. The Super Xiaoai AI Assistant enables more efficient intelligent selection and image application. The ultrasonic fingerprint unlock is fast and secure. It comes standard with a 100W power adapter, data cable, protective case and screen protector, ready to use out of the box. With an innovative rear display, professional Leica imaging and all-round configurations, the Xiaomi 17 Pro Max redefines the new height of flagship smartphones.
