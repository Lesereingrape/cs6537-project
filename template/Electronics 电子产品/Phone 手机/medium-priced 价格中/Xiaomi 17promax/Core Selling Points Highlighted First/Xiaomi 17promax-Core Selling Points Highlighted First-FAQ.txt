Q1: What practical features does the Mi Share Rear Display of the Xiaomi 17 Pro Max have?
A1: The Mi Share Rear Display supports a variety of innovative interaction methods. First, when taking selfies with the rear main camera, the rear display can preview the image in real time, and the imaging quality is greatly improved with the beauty algorithm. Second, the rear display can intuitively show dynamic notifications such as ride-hailing progress, food delivery status, and flight information, making important information clear at a glance. It also supports sticky note and personalized functions: the content selected by Super Xiaoai or photos from the album can be pasted to the rear display with one click, and you can use the avatar sticker template, or even connect to a Mijia printer to print physical photos. In addition, the rear display can act as a control card to switch music, answer calls or control the car remotely (vehicle connection support required). Finally, with the retro handheld game console protective case, the rear display can also run casual mini-games, adding fun to daily life. Starting price is RMB 5,499.

Q2: What Leica-enhanced highlights does its imaging system have?
A2: The rear Leica professional triple camera delivers an outstanding imaging experience. The main camera adopts a 50MP Light Hunter 950L sensor with an f/1.67 large aperture and LOFIC high dynamic range technology (16.5EV), enabling pure and transparent backlight shooting with significantly optimized ghosting and flare. The telephoto lens is a 50MP super light-gathering periscope lens with a 1/2-inch large sensor and an f/2.6 aperture, supporting 5x optical zoom and 30cm telephoto macro, with excellent low-light long-range shooting capability. The ultra-wide-angle lens is also 50MP, with a 102° field of view and 5cm super macro, capable of capturing broad scenes and delicate close-ups. The lens group uses multi-layer coatings such as MgF2, LSC, ALD, PVD and SWC, which effectively eliminate flare and ghosting for more transparent image quality. For portrait photography, the new generation Master Portrait Algorithm driven by ultra-high computing power delivers true and natural skin tones with clear details even in backlight.

Q3: How is the performance and battery life?
A3: In terms of performance, it is equipped with the 5th Generation Snapdragon 8 Elite processor, adopting the 3rd generation 3nm process and 2+6 all big core architecture. The CPU performance is increased by 20%, GPU by 23%, and AI performance by 37%. Combined with LPDDR5X memory and UFS 4.1 flash memory, it can run large applications and games smoothly. In terms of battery life, it has a built-in 7500mAh Xiaomi Jinshajiang Battery with a silicon content of up to 16%, leading in energy density, and the capacity remains ≥80% after 2000 charge cycles. Charging supports 100W wired flash charge (compatible with 100W PPS protocol) and 50W wireless flash charge, and also has a 22.5W reverse charging function. At the same time, the Xiaomi Surge P3 fast charging chip and G2 battery management chip work together to achieve precise battery management, with excellent low-temperature battery life performance and a DOU battery life of up to 1.74 days.
