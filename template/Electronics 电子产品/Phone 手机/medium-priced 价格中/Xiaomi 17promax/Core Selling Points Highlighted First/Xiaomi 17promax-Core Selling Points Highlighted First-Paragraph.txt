The Xiaomi 17 Pro Max breaks the form boundary of smartphones with the Mi Share Rear Display, bringing more wonderful experiences on an extra screen. The rear display not only supports selfie preview, dynamic notifications (ride-hailing/food delivery/flight), sticky notes and personalized wallpapers, but also allows one-click transfer of front display content selected by Super Xiaoai to the rear display, and direct pasting of album photos to the rear display. Matched with a retro handheld game console protective case, the rear display transforms into a casual game console. The control card makes music switching, call answering and remote car control more convenient, integrating innovation into every detail of daily life. In terms of imaging, the Leica professional triple camera unleashes the charm of light and shadow. The 50MP Leica Ultra High Dynamic Range Main Camera adopts the Light Hunter 950L sensor, enhanced by an f/1.67 large aperture and LOFIC high dynamic range technology (16.5EV), enabling pure and transparent backlight shooting. The full-set multi-layer coatings (MgF2/LSC/ALD/PVD/SWC) effectively eliminate ghosting and flare. The 50MP Leica Super Light-Gathering Periscope Telephoto is equipped with a 1/2-inch large sensor, supporting 5x optical zoom and 30cm telephoto macro, greatly improving low-light long-range shooting capability. The 50MP Leica Ultra-Wide-Angle covers a 102° field of view and 5cm super macro, and with the new generation Master Portrait Algorithm, it delivers true skin tones and clear details in backlight, making creative shooting effortless. Performance and battery life are equally powerful. The 5th Generation Snapdragon 8 Elite adopts the 3rd generation 3nm process and 2+6 all big core architecture, with CPU/GPU/AI performance increased by 20%, 23% and 37% respectively. Combined with LPDDR5X and UFS 4.1, it handles multi-tasking and large games with ease. The 7500mAh Xiaomi Jinshajiang Battery has a silicon content of up to 16%, balancing energy density and battery life (≥80% capacity after 2000 charge cycles). It supports 100W Surge Wired Flash Charge (compatible with 100W PPS), 50W Wireless Flash Charge and 22.5W reverse charging. What's more, the dual chips of Surge P3+G2 achieve precise management, with a DOU battery life of up to 1.74 days for all-day worry-free use. The display and design are fully upgraded. The 6.9-inch ultra-narrow bezel large straight display adopts LIPO 3D packaging, and its 2608×1200 super resolution (9.38 million sub-pixels, full RGB arrangement) presents sharp details. The M10 light-emitting system has a 3500nit peak brightness, 1-120Hz adaptive refresh rate, full brightness DC dimming for eye protection, and Xiaomi Dragon Tempered Glass 3.0 for shatter and scratch resistance. Four textured new colors (Forest Green, Misty Purple, White, Black) are matched with a 6-series aerospace aluminum alloy frame and glass fiber back cover, balancing aesthetics and durability. Xiaomi HyperOS 3 brings a smooth full ecosystem experience for people, cars and homes, with upgraded interconnection with Apple devices and faster ultrasonic fingerprint unlock. It comes standard with a 100W fast charging head, data cable and protective case, ready to use out of the box. With an innovative rear display and all-round configurations, the Xiaomi 17 Pro Max defines a new generation of flagship benchmarks, with a starting price of RMB 5,499.
