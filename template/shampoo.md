**【Frequently Asked Questions】**
**Q1: What is the main benefit of this shampoo?**
**A1:** This product focuses on scalp purification and anti-hair loss care. It effectively removes scalp oils and buildup while nourishing hair follicles to strengthen roots and reduce hair fall for fuller, healthier hair.
**Q2: What are the key active ingredients?**
**A2:** It is enriched with Astaxanthin (one of nature’s most powerful antioxidants) and Argan Oil (often called "liquid gold"). Astaxanthin protects hair from UV and oxidative stress, while Argan Oil provides deep moisture to repair split ends and tame frizz.
**Q3: Which hair types is it suitable for?**
**A3:** It is suitable for all hair types, especially fine, flat, or thinning hair. The gentle formula is also safe for color-treated or chemically processed hair.
**Q4: Does it contain sulfates or other irritants?**
**A4:** No. This is a sulfate-free formula (no SLS/SLES). It is also free from parabens, silicones, mineral oils, and artificial colors, making it gentle and non-irritating.
**Q5: How should I use it for the best results?**
**A5:** Apply to wet hair, gently massage into the scalp and through the strands, then rinse thoroughly. For extra nourishment, leave it on for 1-2 minutes before rinsing. It is safe for daily use.
