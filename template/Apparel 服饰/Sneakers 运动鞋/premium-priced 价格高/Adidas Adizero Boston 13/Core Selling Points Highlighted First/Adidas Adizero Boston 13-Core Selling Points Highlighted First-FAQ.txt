Q1: What is the core technology of the ADIZERO BOSTON 13 and how does it help with running?
A1: The core technology is the ENERGYRODS 2.0 biomimetic claw glass fiber columns, inspired by the claw structure of animals. Implanted in the midsole, they provide rigid support, efficiently transmit energy during push-off and deliver powerful propulsive force. Meanwhile, their biomimetic shape flexibly adapts to foot movements when cornering, reducing speed loss. Paired with the dual-density midsole of Lightstrike Pro and Lightstrike 2.0 — the former with an approximately 13.8% increase in material for surging rebound, and the latter acting as a cushioning frame to stabilize the foot feel — it is suitable for full marathon and half marathon training and races. Starting price is RMB 749.

Q2: How is the traction and wear resistance of this shoe?
A2: The outsole features Continental™ ContiGrip rubber, a technology derived from German Continental tires, delivering excellent traction on both wet and dry roads. Combined with the Lightraxion lightweight racing outsole, it enhances overall wear resistance while reducing shoe weight, and can handle various training scenarios such as roads, track and fields, and treadmills.

Q3: What makes its upper and comfort stand out?
A3: The upper is made of Lightweight Mesh material with excellent breathability, preventing stuffiness during long-distance running. Extra padding is added to the tongue and heel areas to improve lockdown and comfort, avoid friction, and let runners focus on their pace. With strong overall lockdown and great stability, it is suitable for daily training and races for both elite and advanced runners.
