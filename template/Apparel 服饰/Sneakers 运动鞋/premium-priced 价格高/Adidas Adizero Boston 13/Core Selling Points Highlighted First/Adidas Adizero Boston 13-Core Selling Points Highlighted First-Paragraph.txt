The Adidas ADIZERO BOSTON 13 is a professional running shoe specially designed for full marathon and half marathon training and races, with a starting price of RMB 749. Inheriting the 45-year championship DNA of the ADIZERO series, it is equipped with the revolutionary ENERGYRODS 2.0 biomimetic claw glass fiber columns, which mimic the mechanical structure of animal claws to form a rigid skeleton in the midsole. It not only delivers powerful propulsive force but also maintains flexible adaptability when cornering, making every step efficient and stable.
The midsole adopts a dual-density system of Lightstrike Pro and Lightstrike 2.0. Among them, the material usage of Lightstrike Pro is increased by approximately 13.8% compared with the previous generation, significantly enhancing the rebound performance; while the Lightstrike 2.0 cushioning frame ensures soft cushioning and stability upon landing, achieving a perfect balance between energy return and cushioning.
For the outsole, the Continental™ ContiGrip rubber and Lightstrike lightweight racing outsole form a powerful combination, providing reliable traction and wear resistance on both wet roads and dry track and fields, allowing runners to focus on their pace. The upper is made of Lightweight Mesh material with upgraded padding on the tongue and heel, bringing skin-friendly and breathable wrapping, while enhancing lockdown to avoid slippage.
This shoe has witnessed the glorious moments of athletes breaking world records in the 2025 London, Berlin, Tokyo and other events, and now brings the professional racing experience to every runner. Whether you are preparing for your first marathon or pursuing a personal best (PB), the BOSTON 13 can be a powerful weapon on your feet.
