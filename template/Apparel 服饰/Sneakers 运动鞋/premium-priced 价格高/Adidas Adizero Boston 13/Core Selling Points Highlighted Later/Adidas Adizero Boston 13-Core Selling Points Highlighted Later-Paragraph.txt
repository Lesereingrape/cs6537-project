Priced from RMB 749, the Adidas ADIZERO BOSTON 13 is a unisex racing running shoe designed for full marathon and half marathon training and races, suitable for various surfaces including roads, track and fields, and treadmills.
The upper is crafted from Lightweight Mesh material for extreme breathability, with additional padding in the tongue and heel areas to enhance wrapping comfort and lockdown, avoiding friction discomfort during long-distance running. The outsole features the Lightraxion lightweight racing outsole combined with the time-tested Continental™ ContiGrip rubber, delivering exceptional traction and wear resistance on both dry and wet surfaces, allowing runners to take every step with confidence.
At the core of its performance, the BOSTON 13 is built with ENERGYRODS 2.0 biomimetic claw glass fiber columns, inspired by the mechanical structure of animal claws. These columns provide rigid propulsion during the push-off phase and adapt to foot flexion when cornering, achieving a perfect balance of flexibility and speed. The midsole adopts a dual-density design of Lightstrike Pro and Lightstrike 2.0: the material usage of Lightstrike Pro is increased by approximately 13.8% compared with the previous generation, delivering more surging energy return; the Lightstrike 2.0 cushioning frame provides solid landing cushioning. The combination of the two creates a stable and responsive running experience.
Inheriting 45 years of meticulous evolution of the ADIZERO series, this shoe has accompanied athletes to break records in major marathons such as London, Berlin and Tokyo in 2025. Now, it brings the championship-level technology to every running enthusiast.
