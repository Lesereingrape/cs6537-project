Q1: What running scenarios is the ADIZERO BOSTON 13 suitable for?
A1: This shoe is specially designed for full marathon and half marathon training and races, and is suitable for road, track and field, and treadmill surfaces. It is an all-round racing training shoe. The starting price is RMB 749.

Q2: What are the characteristics of its upper and outsole materials?
A2: The upper is made of Lightweight Mesh material with excellent breathability, and extra padding is added to the tongue and heel for enhanced comfort and lockdown. The outsole consists of the Lightraxion lightweight racing outsole and Continental™ ContiGrip rubber, balancing light weight, wear resistance and strong traction, ensuring stable performance in all weathers and on all surfaces.

Q3: What is the core technology of this shoe?
A3: The core lies in the ENERGYRODS 2.0 biomimetic claw glass fiber columns, which mimic the structure of animal claws and are embedded in the midsole to provide rigid propulsion during running and flexibility when cornering. It is also paired with a dual-density midsole of Lightstrike Pro (with an approx. 13.8% increase in material) and Lightstrike 2.0, which not only ensures powerful rebound but also stable cushioning, embodying the championship DNA of the ADIZERO series in a concentrated way.
